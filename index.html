What I Do

My goal is to provide you with a safe place to explore issues that might be in the way of a fulfilling life. There is a variety of reasons why people decide to seek therapy. Some are clear and undeniable, others might be more like a sense of unfulfillment. This can happen at any stage of life. I am here to help you explore whatever is that you feel is in the way of a happier and complete life.

Who I Am

I am a registered psychologist with 25 years of experience in counselling and psychotherapy. I completed my studies in Brazil, worked for five years in New Zealand and the last twelve in Melbourne. I completed a post-graduation in Clinical Psychology in Hospital Setting in Brazil and continue my studies in Psychoanalysis to date. I have experience in Mental Health in the public and private sectors, currently working in private practice with individuals and couples.
